MF Quant Analyzer

A free, open, quantitative ranking tool for the Indian direct-growth mutual fund universe.

Download:

📊 Live Workbook (.xlsm, one-click refresh) 📄 Snapshot Workbook (.xlsx, no macros) 🔗 Raw Data (JSON)

Updated daily. ~1,600+ funds across 16 classifications, ranked within category using a multi-factor quantitative score (Returns, Risk, Consistency, Regime, Efficiency) — fully adjustable weights, transparent methodology, no login required.
